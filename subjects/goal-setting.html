Goal Setting

Setting goals can help an organization get to where it wants to be. Learn how setting goals can help align efforts, communicate objectives, and help teams achieve more.

From New Year's resolutions to quarterly quotas, goal setting is something individuals and organizations often do to both achieve great new things and ensure steady, measured progress.

Carefully setting goals, whether using Objective and Key Results (OKRs) like Google or some other method, can help a team understand what it is working toward and how progress will be tracked and measured. And research has shown that committing to a goal can help improve performance.
